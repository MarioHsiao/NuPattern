1. Open the Guidance document in Word
2. Expand all sub documents (Review | Outline | Show Subdocuments)
3. Hit CTRL+HOME to goto the first line of the document (the Title line)
4. Hit END to goto the end of the first line
5. Hit ENTER tocreate an empty paragraph
6. Insert a standard Table Of Contents, ensure you set for Level 4 topics, you can choose the 'Simple' style, and choose dots for the separators
7. Save as PDF file in same location, with the same name
8. Close the document an DO NOT save.
